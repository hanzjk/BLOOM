About

Let's Bloom

Bloom is a free web application which is designed to relieve stress and anxiety. You can select a stress relieveing method by yourself or you can use the scan option. Once you click on the scan option, you get the most prefered method that suits your current mood. The main aim is to relax your mind and relieve the stress.
