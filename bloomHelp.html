HOW TO USE BLOOM

  • Home

Bloom is a free web application designed to relieve stress and anxiety. There are 3 main features offered for you. You can either select a feature by yourself or you can use the Face Scan option. Once you click the Face Scan option, we will detect your face using your device's camera and then analyze it to suggest the most suitable feature according to your current mood.

    • HOW CAN YOU GET THE BEST EXPERINCE FROM BLOOM

    Is it overwhelming to choose a feature? Don't worry we got you!
  • First, choose the Face Scan option. You'll be directed to a page as shown below



  • Then, click the start button and grant permission for us to access your device's camera.

  • Now, wait for few seconds for us to detect your face. Using the latest technologies of machine learning, our program will analyze your mood and suggest the most suitable feature accordingly.


  • You can either choose the feature we recommend for you by clicking on its name or choose a feature on your own, It's completly upto you!
