HOW TO USE THE MUSIC PLAYER

Music can have a profound effect on your mind and body. Upbeat music can make you feel more optimistic and positive about life whilst a slower tempo can quiet your mind and relax your muscles, making you feel soothed while releasing the stress of the day. Music is effective for relaxation and stress management.


    What makes our music player so special?

    Usually, the user has to manually browse through the playlist to select a song. Here we are proposing an efficient and accurate model, that would suggest a playlist based on your current emotional state.


    HOW WE DETECT YOUR MOOD

    The human face has an advanced mechanical structure. It plays a major role in extraction of an individual's emotional state. Here's how our music player capture your emotion:

  • First, we will detect your face using your device's camera. In order to do this you have to grant us permission to access your web camera.

  • Then, using machine learning techniques BLOOM will analyse your face to detect your current mood.

  • Moods are categorized into 5 main emotions. Happy, Sad, Angry, Fear and Neutral. BLOOM will suggest you a playlist according to the detected emotion.

  • PLEASE note that since this is a real-time process and we will not store any of your photos or videos. Refer the following steps to clear any doubts on using the music player

    • STEP 1: Click on the music player option under features and you will be directed to a page as shown below. Click on the camera as instructed and grant permission for BLOOM to access your web camera or click no-camera button to choose a mood without a face scan.



    STEP 2: Once you click the camera button BLOOM will show you the detected mood. You can either click OK and move on to listen to the playlist or you can neglect it by clicking NO and manually select the mood.



    STEP 3: If you neglect the BLOOM's detected mood or if you neglect the face scan at the begining, you can manually select any mood from the 5 moods shown below which consists 15 songs in each mood.



    STEP 4: Then you'll be directed to Music Player page and click the play button to hear the songs. After listening to the songs for a while our chat bot will appear to check on you. You can either have a chat with it(if you want to chat with him more use the Chat Bot option) and keep listening to the tracks or ask it to suggest another feature



    STEP 5: You can select different tracks from the playlist by clicking the menu icon as shown below.
